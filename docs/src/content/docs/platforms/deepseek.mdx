DeepSeek is known for its strong reasoning capabilities, including the DeepSeek-R1 model's chain-of-thought reasoning. HAEVN captures DeepSeek's thinking traces alongside the final responses.

## Supported URLs

- `chat.deepseek.com`

## What gets synced

| Content | Supported |
|---|---|
| Text messages | ✅ |
| **Reasoning / thinking traces** | ✅ |
| Code blocks | ✅ |
| Mathematical notation | ✅ |

### Thinking traces

DeepSeek's reasoning models produce visible thinking traces — intermediate reasoning steps before the final answer. HAEVN captures these traces and displays them in the Viewer as collapsible sections, similar to Claude's extended thinking blocks.

<Aside type="tip">
  DeepSeek's thinking traces can be long — sometimes longer than the final response itself. HAEVN preserves them in full, making it possible to study how the model approached a problem.
</Aside>

## Known limitations

- **Images** — image generation and uploads are not currently supported.
- **Deep Research mode** — deep research responses are captured as text; inline source citations may not render with full formatting.
