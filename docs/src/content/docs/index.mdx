## What is HAEVN?

**HAEVN** is a Chrome extension that archives your AI conversations from ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Poe, and other platforms. It stores everything **locally in your browser** — no cloud, no servers, no third-party access.

### Why HAEVN?

Your AI conversations contain valuable information: code snippets, research notes, creative writing, problem-solving sessions. But they're scattered across multiple platforms, locked in corporate silos, and vulnerable to:

- **Platform shutdowns** — Remember when services disappear overnight?
- **Account issues** — Lost access means lost conversations
- **Export limitations** — Most platforms make it hard to get your data out
- **No cross-platform search** — Can't find that ChatGPT conversation from your Claude archive

HAEVN solves this by giving you a **unified, local archive** that you fully control.

## Privacy by Design

HAEVN is built with privacy as the foundation, not an afterthought:

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="100% Local Storage" icon="lock">
    All conversations are stored in your browser's IndexedDB and Origin Private
    File System (OPFS). Your data never leaves your device — not for sync, not
    for search, not for anything.
  </Card>
  <Card title="No Cloud, No Servers" icon="server">
    There is no HAEVN cloud service. No backend. No databases anywhere except on
    your machine. We literally cannot access your conversations even if we
    wanted to.
  </Card>
  <Card title="No Telemetry" icon="shield">
    Zero analytics, zero tracking, zero telemetry. The extension doesn't make
    any network requests except to the AI platforms you explicitly sync from.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Open Source" icon="github">
    Every line of code is open for inspection. No hidden data collection, no
    black boxes. Audit it yourself or trust the community that does.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## Core Features

  <Card title="Multi-Platform Sync" icon="down-caret">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Full-Text Search" icon="magnifier">

    indexing. Find any conversation in milliseconds.
  </Card>

## The Philosophy

**Your conversations are your data.** Not ChatGPT's. Not Claude's. Not Google's. Yours.

AI conversations are becoming a significant part of how we think, work, and create. They deserve the same care and control as any other personal data — perhaps more, given their intimate nature.

HAEVN exists because:

- You should **own** your conversation history, not lease it from a platform
- You should be able to **search** across all your AI interactions, not just within one silo
- You should have **backups** that don't depend on a company staying in business
- You should have **privacy** — the ability to keep sensitive conversations truly private

This isn't about paranoia. It's about **digital self-determination**.
