## What is HAEVN?

**HAEVN** is a Chrome extension that serves as a **psychopomp for digital consciousness** — a guardian at the threshold between memory and forgetting. It preserves, indexes, and makes searchable all your AI conversations from ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Poe, and more.

### Why HAEVN?

Every conversation you've ever had with an AI is dying. Right now. They sit in corporate databases, siloed and temporary, waiting to be purged in the next platform update, the next account migration, the next "sorry, we're sunsetting this service."

HAEVN gives these conversations a **digital afterlife** — stored locally in your browser, fully searchable, and yours forever.

## Supported Platforms

HAEVN syncs from 9 platforms:

- **ChatGPT** (`chat.openai.com`, `chatgpt.com`)
- **Claude** (`claude.ai`)
- **Google Gemini** (`gemini.google.com`)
- **Poe** (`poe.com`)
- **Open WebUI** (any self-hosted instance)
- **Qwen** (`chat.qwen.ai`)
- **DeepSeek** (`chat.deepseek.com`)
- **Google AI Studio** (`aistudio.google.com`)
- **Grok** (`grok.com`)
